Instructions

In this study you will play three rounds of "Matching Pennies".

At the beginning, you will be randomly assigned to an opponent and will keep playing against that same opponent in all three rounds.

Player 1 chooses either "Heads" or "Tails". Player 2 guesses what Player 1 chose.

  • If the guess is correct, Player 2 receives 100 points and Player 1 receives nothing.
  • If the guess is incorrect, Player 1 receives 100 points and Player 2 receives nothing.

For your convenience, these instructions will remain available to you on all subsequent screens of this study.
